Tár (stylized as TÁR) is a 2022 drama film directed, written, and produced by Todd Field. 
The film follows the life and downfall of a renowned female composer-conductor, portrayed by Cate Blanchett, in her pursuit of maintaining her status and image. 
Tár had its world premiere at the 79th Venice International Film Festival in September 2022, where Blanchett won the Volpi Cup for Best Actress. 
The film received widespread critical acclaim, with reviewers lauding Blanchett's performance, Field's screenplay and direction, cinematography, and score.
